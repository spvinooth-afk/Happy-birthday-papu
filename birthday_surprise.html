✦ From my heart to yours ✦

A letter written just for you

My dearest,

I sat down to write this a hundred times and couldn't find the right words. Because how do you put into language what someone means to you when they've become such a part of how you see the world?

You came into my life and you made it quieter and louder at the same time. Quieter, because with you there's a kind of peace I hadn't known. Louder, because everything feels more vivid — more alive — when you're in it.

I have faced all the versions of the world that exist without you in my life, and I am so grateful I don't have to live in any of them anymore. I get this version. The one where I wake up and you exist. The one where I get to be loved by you.

On your birthday, I want you to know this: you deserve every beautiful thing. Every soft morning. Every belly laugh. Every dream you're chasing. You deserve the world offered to you gently, with both hands.

I hope today is the beginning of your most beautiful year yet — full of everything your heart quietly hopes for, and a few surprises that take your breath away.

Thank you for being you. Thank you for letting me love you. Thank you for being here.

Forever yours ❤️
